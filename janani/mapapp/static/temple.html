kammalakanni amman temple

Local tradition holds that the shrine of Senjiamman / Kamalakanni Amman may precede even the fort itself, with its development tracing back to around 1200 CE, when Ananda Kon, a leader of the Konar community (shepherds), is said to have established early structures in this region. There is an important festival of 10 days held annually for Kamalakanni Amman. Car (chariot) processions are part of the events.
